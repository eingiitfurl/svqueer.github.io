We believe that having a designated safe space is crucial for individuals in our community, as it provides a sense of security and support.

Our classroom is a welcoming and inclusive environment for all individuals. We encourage our members to use this space as a place to relax, connect with others, and find some rest and support from the challenges they may face on a daily basis.

We are committed to creating a safe and supportive environment for all members of our community, and we invite you to join us in our efforts to promote inclusivity and equality.

Are you ready to slay at our fun parties and events? Come as you are and be your fabulous self in a space free of judgement and full of acceptance. We know how important it is to have a place where you can let loose, connect with like-minded individuals, and be free to express yourself without fear of discrimination.

Our events are designed to create an environment where you can let your hair down, dance, and connect with other fabulous humans.

So come join us and let's party! Let's slay together and create unforgettable memories in a welcoming and inclusive community.

Our association provides a safe space for you to connect with other queer people, talk about all things LGBTQIA+ and join our thriving community in Deventer. Whether you want to have fun at our parties or engage in thought-provoking discussions at our educational events, we've got you covered. Our community is all about intersectionality, visibility, acceptance and celebrating all different identities. You are welcome to join us and be your authentic self without judgment.

Come and join our queer fam and let's create positive change together!

Our members not only have the opportunity to connect with queer-friendly businesses and internships but also receive a discount at vintage clothing store Cream in Deventer. s.v. QUEER! strives to create a supportive and safe environment for individuals who identify as queer or allies. By providing resources and connections, the association helps members feel proud and empowered to be themselves.

s.v. QUEER! hosts educational events that promote understanding and acceptance, with a focus on LGBTQIA+ topics. Members have the opportunity to learn, connect, and engage in friendly discussions with like-minded individuals in a safe and supportive environment.

These discussions provide a space for individuals to share their experiences and perspectives, ask questions, and challenge their own biases. By creating a community of acceptance and understanding, the association is working towards a world where everyone is valued and celebrated, regardless of their gender identity or sexual orientation.

Ready to join a community that celebrates diversity and promotes inclusivity? Look no further than s.v. QUEER! You'll have the opportunity to connect with others and create lifelong friendships in a safe and supportive environment. Whether you identify as queer or an ally, our association is open to all who share our commitment to equality and acceptance. So what are you waiting for? Join s.v. QUEER!
